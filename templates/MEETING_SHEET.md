# Meeting sheet

Use this file for cross-lane communication. Managers own the file. Workers may
append rows only for their own lane or when their manager grants a short lock in
`TEAM_PLAN.md`.

| Time | From | To | Topic | Request / decision | Linked checklist | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `<UTC>` | `<lane>` | `<lane/manager/GM>` | `<topic>` | `<question, decision, handoff, blocker>` | `<TEAM_PLAN row>` | open |

Keep this file under 120 lines. Managers should archive resolved rows to
`docs/parallel-sessions/archive/meeting_sheet-<date>.md` before the sheet becomes
expensive for new panes to read.
